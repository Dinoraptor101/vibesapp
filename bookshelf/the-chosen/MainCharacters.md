The Chosen features biblical characters in various roles across the narrative.​

Main Characters
The core characters include:​

Jesus - Central figure and teacher

Simon Peter - Fisherman and apostle

Mary Magdalene - Follower freed from demonic possession

Matthew - Former tax collector, apostle

Andrew - Fisherman, Simon Peter's brother, apostle

John - Apostle

Big James - Apostle

Little James - Apostle

Thomas - Apostle

Thaddeus - Apostle

Nathanael - Apostle

Simon Z (Simon the Zealot) - Apostle

Judas Iscariot - Apostle

Philip - Apostle

Supporting Characters
Key recurring characters include:​

Mary of Nazareth (Mother Mary) - Jesus' mother

Nicodemus - Pharisee

Shmuel - Religious leader

Gaius - Roman centurion

Quintus - Roman official

Atticus Aemilius - Roman official

Joseph Ben Caiaphas - High priest

Herod Antipas - Tetrarch of Galilee

Pontius Pilate - Roman governor
