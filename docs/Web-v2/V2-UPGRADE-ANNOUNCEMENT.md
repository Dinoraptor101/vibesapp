# VibesApp V2: Coming Soon

## A Message from the Creator

Working on Vibes started as a dream to create a localised community—essentially a social network in reverse where the purpose is nothing about media or fame or jokes, but to cultivate real-life connections with people. I have learned many things from building a project from the literal "scratch". I understood what went wrong and what went right, but most importantly I understood that we need a platform such as this.

That said, security and safety are fundamental to having "Fun"—and that's why this project will only be good if real-life people join, share real things, and maintain one person per account policy.

With V2, we're rebuilding from the ground up with everything we've learned. Here's what's changing:

---

## 🔄 What We're Saying Goodbye To

### Public Group Chats on Posts
The old public group chat system where anyone could join a conversation on a post is being retired. While it was an interesting experiment, we found that open group chats fragmented conversations and made moderation challenging.

**Group chats aren't gone—they're evolving.** We're exploring a more intentional approach with to create spaces that feel more like gathering with neighbours than shouting into a crowd. More details coming as we develop this feature.

### Old Activity History
Your old notifications and activity feed won't carry over to V2. Think of this as a fresh start with a cleaner, more organised notification system built from the ground up.

### Vibes Scoring (Temporarily)
The complex vibes scoring system is being redesigned and won't be available at launch. We're rethinking how community reputation works to make it more transparent and less gamified. **This is coming soon** once we get it right.

### Like/Dislike Buttons
The dual like/dislike system is gone. We realised mixing positive engagement with negative feedback created friction. but you can heart and report (see below).

## 🎉 What's New & Improved

### Comments with Reactions
Posts now have a **full comment section**. Have real conversations in near-realtime, reply to specific comments, and react with hearts, and replies are threaded.

### Report Button + Human Moderation
Instead of a dislike button, we have a **dedicated report system**. When content crosses the line (pornography, spam, hate speech), you can report it. Reports are reviewed by both community members and staff oversight, with real consequences for violations.

### Hearts-Only Engagement
No more negative vibes. You can **heart posts and comments** to show appreciation. That's it. Positive only. If something's genuinely problematic, use the report button. We're separating celebration from moderation.

### Accessibility for Everyone
V2 is **100% WCAG AA compliant**. Full screen reader support, keyboard navigation, proper color contrast, reduced motion options. If you need accessibility features, they're built-in from day one—not bolted on later.

### Massively Faster Performance
- 83% faster load times
- 95% faster hot reloads during development
- 37% smaller download size
- Works offline as a Progressive Web App (Read only mode)

### Three Beautiful Themes
We carried over Aleksei's Light, Dim, and Dark modes — all designed with proper contrast and accessibility in mind. Pick what feels right for your eyes.
