# Project Overview

## Mission Statement

VibesApp is a **social network without the media nonsense** designed to connect real people together and foster wholesome content through community self-moderation and a private vibes-driven reputation system.

## Core Principles

### 🌍 Location-Based Community

- **100-mile interaction radius** - Users can only interact with posts from people within 100 miles
- **Local focus** - Encourages real-world community building and authentic connections
- **Privacy-first** - General location only, no real-time tracking

### 🎯 Merit-Based Permissions

- **Vibes system** - Private reputation score that unlocks features
- **Earned privileges** - Higher vibes = more capabilities (group chat, DM, advanced features)
- **Community self-moderation** - Users collectively determine content quality

### 🚫 Anti-Social Media Problems

- **No advertisements** - Clean, user-focused experience
- **No bots** - Merit system prevents automated spam
- **No algorithms** - Chronological, location-based feed
- **No infinite scroll toxicity** - Thoughtful engagement over addiction

## Platform Goals

### Short-term Objectives

- Build authentic local communities
- Establish effective self-moderation through vibes system
- Create positive engagement patterns
- Achieve sustainable user growth

### Long-term Vision

- Become the go-to platform for local community building
- Demonstrate viability of merit-based social networks
- Expand globally while maintaining local focus
- Influence industry toward healthier social media models

## Target Audience

### Primary Users

- **Community-minded individuals** seeking authentic local connections
- **Young adults (18-35)** comfortable with location-based services
- **Quality-over-quantity** users who prefer thoughtful engagement
- **Privacy-conscious** individuals wanting alternatives to big tech platforms

### Use Cases

- Discovering local events and activities
- Finding like-minded people nearby
- Sharing local recommendations and insights
- Building neighborhood communities
- Organizing local meetups and activities

## Competitive Advantage

### Technical Innovation

- **Merit-based permission system** - Novel approach to preventing spam/abuse
- **Location-proximity reactions** - Only nearby users affect your vibes
- **Progressive feature unlocking** - Gamification through earned capabilities
- **Community-driven content moderation** - Automated hiding based on community feedback

### User Experience

- **Mobile-first design** - Progressive Web App capabilities
- **Clean, distraction-free interface** - Focus on content and connections
- **Meaningful interactions** - Vibes cost encourages thoughtful posting
- **Real-time features** - Group chat and direct messaging for active users
